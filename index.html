RECYCLING GUIDE

What Can Be Recycled?

A quick guide to help you recycle correctly and reduce contamination in our recycling streams.

Paper & Cardboard

Accepted

  • Newspapers & magazines
  • Office paper
  • Cardboard boxes
  • Paper bags

Avoid

  • Waxed paper
  • Pizza boxes with grease

Plastics

Accepted

  • Water bottles
  • Food containers
  • Milk jugs
  • Detergent bottles

Avoid

  • Plastic bags
  • Styrofoam
  • Bubble wrap

Glass

Accepted

  • Bottles
  • Jars
  • Food containers

Avoid

  • Window glass
  • Mirrors
  • Light bulbs

Quick Recycling Tips

Empty & Rinse

Clean containers before recycling to prevent contamination.

Remove Lids

Separate lids from containers for proper processing.

Don't Bag Recyclables

Place items loose in your recycling bin, not in plastic bags.

When in Doubt, Throw it Out

If you're unsure, it's better to dispose of it as trash.
